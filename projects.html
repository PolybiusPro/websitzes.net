Projects

Furtrieve

Furtrieve is a business that provides easy tracking through the app, so that owners of service animals and family pets will have peace of mind. During my time at Furtrieve I managed the website and SEO; increasing revenue and completely remodeling the website, in turn elevating traffic. I also worked along side other junior developers in testing and programming the GPS dog collars to ensure safety and customer satisfaction. While multitasking Furtrieve and the collars, I also worked on IT maintenance; ensuring our team had no delay in the developmental process. I also implemented a local network attached storage so that coworkers could share files safely and efficiently. Currently at Furtrieve, I am doing routine maintenance, updates, and guiding new interns.

Aubry Lane

Aubry Lane was created alongside Furtrieve with the purpose of implementing tracking devices for the safety and peace of mind of women and children. They offer over one hundred different designer bags that are equipped with a concealed pocket for the small personal tracking device that can easily controlled with the app. During my time with Aubry Lane, I worked on the front and back-end of the site, re-building it from the ground up.
